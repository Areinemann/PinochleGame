Common actions that a player can do:

-play a card by clicking on a card (card will have onClick method)
-make a bid for the points they predict their hand would earn
-declare a trump suit when they have the highest bid(game interface will prompt player)
-draw a card from deck (random card will be chosen from array of unplayed cards)
-make a meld with existing cards (game will pause until all the cards user wants for the 				meld are in play, so their turn is not interrupted)
-see possible melds, by clicking on the hint button, labelled with ‘?’
-play a trick in the second round 
-rotate screen 180 degrees to play (game will only be played on landscape layout)
-minimize game screen
-adjust screen settings on home screen
